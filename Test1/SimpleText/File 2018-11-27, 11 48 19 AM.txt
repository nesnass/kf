By promoting the interaction between professionals, you are causing a greater change
of the professional frame of reference and the professional context. Understanding each other and
speaking a common language within the professional context is an important part of
the learning. This way, the creation of new knowledge together with others is made possible in the
professional practice. In the knowledge creation metaphor, Paavola et al., (2004) describe each other
fully understand "as an essential part of learning. The professional context is called Wenger
(2009) a Community of Practice (CoP). It is true professionals with each other, already
discovering together and designing something new on that basis.
